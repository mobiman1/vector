# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a welcoming experience for everyone, regardless of experience level, background, or identity.

## Our Standards

**Encouraged:**
- Helping others learn and get unstuck
- Giving constructive, specific feedback
- Focusing on what's best for the community
- Being direct and respectful

**Not acceptable:**
- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks
- Dismissing others' questions or contributions
- Sharing others' private information without consent
- Any conduct that would be inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the maintainers via [Discord](https://discord.gg/rkU8UTu7dY) or the email listed in [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md). All complaints will be reviewed and responded to promptly. Maintainers have the right to remove, edit, or reject any contributions or ban participants who violate this code of conduct.
